Truth or Dare Randi

How to play

  1. Choose the number of players that must play
  2. Only 2 to 6 playes can be chosen
  3. Once number of players has been chosen, depending on the number of players buttons will be displayed
  4. Each player can choose their box by clicking it, the'll be given a random color
  5. All must choose their box to play the game
  6. Truth or Dare button will give you the randomly selected Dare or Truth
  7. If the dare or truth is completed just press complete
  8. Reset the game if you want
